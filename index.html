About Me

I bring the hard work, determination, and relentlessness from my software sales experience to the world of development.

I am a front-end software developer proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React. My experience working in customer-facing roles has really allowed me to take the end-users experience into consideration when building my applications.

My passion for building sites and apps has helped push me to continuously learn new skills and add new technologies to my toolbag. As a lifelong learner, the endless possibilities of web development excites me and confirms that I am in the right place.
