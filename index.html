About Me

I bring the hard work, determination, and relentlessness from my software sales experience to the world of development.

I am a front-end software developer proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React. My experience working in customer-facing roles has really allowed me to take the end-users experience into consideration when building my applications.

My passion for building sites and apps has helped push me to continuously learn new skills and add new technologies to my toolbag. As a lifelong learner, the endless possibilities of web development excites me and confirms that I am in the right place.

Outside of my dev work, my life is filled with consuming everything Star Wars, weekly trips to the tennis courts, cooking and eating new food, and being miserable from yet another disappointing Leafs season.

My Story

After four years in sales, I realized that staying in sales was no longer my long-term goal, and I wanted to find a role that kept me more engaged throughout the day.

I was fortunate to have had the opportunity to sell to so many incredible startup founders across Canada, which led to my love of the tech world. Learning how to code and build my own products was something that always appealed to me, so when I decided to make a career change, jumping into web development was an easy choice.

In January 2021, after a couple of months of part-time learning and research, I made the decision to leave my Account Executive role and pursue coding full-time.

I enrolled in Juno College's Accelerated Web Development and JavaScript courses, and then their immersive Web Development Bootcamp, where I learned how to create accessible and responsive sites and apps using React, SCSS, JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS3.

The skills and tools that I picked up during the bootcamp have really helped me see the endless possibilities of what I can build. There is so much to learn with web development and that could not excite me more, because I am just getting started.
